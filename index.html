What is Gymmigo?

Gymmigo is a user-friendly app designed to streamline the process of borrowing gym equipment in universities. Say goodbye to paper logs and hello to digital convenience.

Key Features

Instant Equipment Reservation

Reserve gym equipment like sports balls with a few taps, whenever you need them.

Real-Time Equipment Availability

Check availability of gym equipment instantly, no need to check with the staff.

Borrowing Notifications

Receive notifications about your reservation status

Easy Secure Login

Login using Google to quickly access your equipment borrowing history and reservations.
